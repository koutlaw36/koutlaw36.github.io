Parks

Magic Kingdom

Fairytale dreams come true for children of all ages at Magic Kingdom park. Delight in classic attractions, enchanting fireworks, musical parades and beloved Disney Characters across 6 themed lands. Zoom through space, become a swashbuckling pirate and watch fireworks light up the sky over Cinderella Castle.

Epcot

Explore exciting attractions, enchanting international pavilions, award-winning fireworks and seasonal special events. Celebrating the human spirit, Epcot has 2 distinct realms: Future World, whch features technological innovations, and World Showcase, which shares with Guests the culture and cuisine of 11 countries: Mexico, Norway, China, Germany, Italy, the United States, Japan, Morocco, France, United Kingdom and Canada.

Hollywood Studios

Movie magic comes to life at Disney's Hollywood Studios, awash in the glitz and glamour of Hollywood's Golden Age. Step into the action with attractions based on blockbuster movies--including Star Wars--and top TV shows, and delight in exciting entertainment that puts you at center stage. With the vibrancy of a bustling movie set, this park features 7 sections including Hollywood Boulevard, Muppet Courtyard, Sunset Boulevard, and Pixar Palace.

Animal Kingdom

Encounter the magic of nature with rare creatures, authentic adventures and world-class entertainment at Disney's Animal Kingdom park, one of hte largest animal theme parks in the world. Home to more than 2,000 animals across 300 species, the park celebrates the beauty, mystery and harmony of all living things. Explore 6 enthralling lands, each one alive with magic like nowhere else.

Blizzard Beach

Discover frosty fun for the whole family at Disney's Blizzard Beach water park, a one-time ski resort that has melted into a watery wonderland.

  • Zip down the slushy slopes of Mount Gushmore on one of the world's tallest and fastest waterslides.
  • Float down the tranquil river and sunbathe on the white-sand beach.

Typhoon Lagoon

Escape to Disney's Typhoon Lagoon water park for a storm of fun in the sun! After an epic typhoon hurled surfboards into palm trees and tossed boats like toys, the storm-soaked Mount Mayday became a topsy-turvy oasis of water-filled adventure.

  • Snorkel with sharks, stingrays and tropical fish in the coral reef.
  • Plunge down rushing rapids, sunbathe on the sandy beach and glide down the lazy river on a relaxing raft ride.

Disney Springs

Disney Springs features an eclectic mix of unique boutiques, one-of-a-kind eateries and jaw-dropping entertainment that will have you wondering where the day went. Savor a variety of culinary delights among a collection of waterside cafes and divine restaurants. Wander through vibrant walkways and uncover fashionable finds inside more than 50 shops, boutiques, and kiosks.

Resorts

Deluxe Resort Hotels

Disney's Grand Floridian Resort & Spa

Victorian elegance meets modern sophistication at this lavish Disney Resort hotel. Relax in the sumptuous lobby as the live orchestra plays ragtime, jazz and popular Disney tunes.

  • Pool with Waterslide
  • Exquisite Dining
  • World-Class Spa & Health Club
  • Magic Kingdom Resort Area

Disney's Beach Club

Discover the casual elegance of this charming, New England-style Resort hotel. Savor exquisite dining, and explore the pools, lagoons and spas of Stormalong Bay.

  • Cool off with a lazy river, waterslide, quiet pools, whirlpool spas and more at Stormalong Bay.
  • Stay in shape at our complimentary state-of-the-art fitness center.
  • Epcot Resort Area

Disney's Polynesian Village Resort

Celebrate the spirit of the South Pacific at this oasis of tropical palms and lush vegetation. Enjoy moonlit nights immersed in the outdoor island ambience and the exotic tastes of our divine restaurants.

  • Delight in an all-you-care-to-enjoy, family-style luau with hula dancing and a fire-knife performer!
  • Magic Kindgom Resort Area

Disney's Boardwalk Inn

Experience the charm and whimsy of turn-of-the-century Atlantic City at this waterfront Resort hotel. Discover carnival games, unique dining and exciting nightlife on the Coney Island-style boardwalk.

  • Make a splash at Luna Park Pool.
  • Delight in a massage at the state-of-the-art fitness center.
  • Epcot Resort Area

Disney's Wilderness Lodge

Escape to the rustic majesty of America's Great Northwest. Inspired by turn-of-the-century National Park lodges, Disney's Wilderness Lodge celebrates American craftmanship and honors the beauty of the untamed wildnerness.

  • Enjoy nature trails through pine forests and rocking chairs that overlook a murmuring creek.
  • Discover a whimsical heated pool surrounded by pine trees and boulders.
  • Magic Kingdom Resort Area

Disney's Contemporary Resort

Retreat to this ultra-modern Disney Resort hotel and discover award-winning dining, spectacular views and dazzling pools.

  • Savor one of the best fireworks dining experiences at Walt Disney World Resort at California Grill.
  • Cool off as you take your pick from 2 pools located along the shore of Bay Lake.
  • Magic Kingdom Resort Area

Disney's Animal Kingdom Lodge

Step into the heart of Africa at this magnificent Resort hotel. Inspired by the traditional African kraal, the horsehoe-curved design provides spectacular views of 4 lush savannas that over 200 hoofed animals and birds call home.

  • Escape to an 11,000-square-foot pool with an exciting waterslide and gently sloping, zero-depth entry point similar to that of a natural watering hole.
  • Savor vibrant African specialties and favorite American classics at our award-winning restaurants.
  • Animal Kingdom Resort Area
